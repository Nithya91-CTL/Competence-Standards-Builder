Competence Standards Builder

Assess your course information against competence standards guidance and generate V1 and V2 standards.

1. Enter information 2. Draft standards 3. Prompt questions 4. Version 2 and guidance
1

Enter course information

Add the course or programme name, level and the information you want assessed. This can include summative assessments, course learning outcomes and teaching modules.

Reference lens from the template

  • Identify what all students must demonstrate to complete the course successfully.
  • Distinguish competence standards from assessment conditions and administrative requirements.
  • Check that each standard is relevant, justified and proportionate.
  • Separate what is being assessed from how it is assessed.
  • State speaking, listening, handwriting or timed performance only where genuinely essential.
